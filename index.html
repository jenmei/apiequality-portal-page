API Equality

Welcome to APIEquality.org, a statewide collaboration of API Equality-LA and API Equality-Northern California, two of the leading organizations advocating and organizing for fairness and equality at the intersection of the Asian Pacific Islander (API) and Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, and Queer (LGBTQ) communities.

As grassroots organizations, API Equality-LA and API Equality-Northern California are committed to building a strong base of supporters and volunteers in our regions. Statewide, we are jointly working to strengthen and expand our statewide network of LGBTQ API activists and allies and present a powerful and unified front in support of just policies for the API and LGBTQ communities and in defense against anti-LGBT efforts in the state.

This portal is just the beginning of our statewide coordination efforts with new updates and a virtual technical assistance and training center underway.
